Public Safety Tips

Road Damage / Potholes

Drive slowly near damaged roads and avoid sudden braking. Report potholes to local municipal authorities to prevent accidents.

Garbage Dump

Avoid areas with large garbage dumps as they may spread infections. Report waste accumulation to the municipal sanitation department.

Broken Street Lights

Dark streets increase the risk of accidents and crime. Inform the electricity department about non-functional street lights.

Water Leakage

Leaking pipelines can damage roads and waste water resources. Report leaks to the local water supply authority.
