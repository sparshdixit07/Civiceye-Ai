Privacy Policy

At CivicEye AI, we value user privacy and transparency. This page explains how user data is handled.

Image Data

Images uploaded by users are processed only for AI hazard analysis and are not permanently stored on the platform.

AI Processing

Image analysis is performed using AI models to detect potential civic safety hazards.

No Personal Data Collection

CivicEye AI does not collect personal information such as name, phone number or address.

Third Party Services

AI processing may involve external AI services for generating analysis results.

Security

We strive to maintain the security and privacy of all user interactions on the platform.
